LGames

This repo contains different little games. You can have a try and experience the game.

  1. LBoxShooter Try to shoot boxes to win the game. When you lose or beat the game level, shoot the rotating text to continue.
  2. LMadnessRoller Control the roller ball to defeat the level, good luck.
